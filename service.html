Our Roles

Explore Our Roles

Chairperson

Presides at meetings of the Executive Council, AGM, and Special General Meeting; Head of ZUSA and subject to the constitution, answerable and liable to all matters pertaining ZUSA; Nominates the legal affairs secretary with the approval of the members of the Executive Council and the ZUSA Congress. Represents ZUSA at the University Council, Senate and the Students Disciplinary Committee, unless he/she is the subject of a disciplinary hearing; Articulates the policies of ZUSA to the senate and University council, ZUSA Congress and provide exemplary leadership Co-signatory in all ZUSA cheques and other negotiable transactions for the purpose of withdrawals; An ex-official member of all ad hoc Committees including Congress committees Responsible for the overall administration of the executive council and the association; Make visits to all ZETECH University campuses at least twice a month and give report to dean of students. Address the first meeting of Congress after the election of the speaker and every first sitting of Congress in every semester;

Secretary General

Is the Secretary of ZUSA executive council and the Association; Executive council’s official spokesperson, communicate the executive council’s decisions to the members, ZUSA Congress and Zetech University’s Administration; Takes minutes of all executive council meetings and the Association’s general meetings; Responsible for all general correspondence on behalf of the association Keeps all records and documents of the association including keeping files, registers, financial records and minutes of ZUSA Ensure the accurate and timely dissemination of information relevant to ZUSA, and supervise all official publications; With the authorization of the Vice-Chancellor, the Secretary-General may make public statements to the media and institutions outside the University. In consultation with the chairperson, communicate to all members executive council members the venue, dates and agendas of executive meetings or General Meeting as the case may be; Keep an accurate register of members and make it available for purposes of dissolution, vote of no confidence, elections and other purposes; In consultation with the chairperson draw the ZUSA program of activities for the semester and in the first sitting of Congress present the proposal for approval in ZUSA Congress; Is co-signatory to all ZUSA instruments and/ or documents with the chairperson. Chairperson of external Affairs Committee Is the public relations officer of ZUSA Identify, propose and advise ZUSA through the executive on the external links Keep records of communications with external links and bodies Organize for trips and field works for the ZUSA members Is answerable to ZUSA Congress on external matters

Finance Secretary

Keep true, accurate, and timely records of the proceedings of every financial transaction of ZUSA; Custodian of all ZUSA financial records and ensure that ZUSA’s books of accounts are safe; Ensures that every payment and expenditure is duly authorized; A mandatory signatory of the ZUSA cheques and negotiable instruments for the purpose of withdrawal or deposits. Is in charge of resource mobilization and income generating activities and projects of the association; Draws the annual budget of ZUSA with the finance committee and table it before the executive counciland ZUSA Congress for approval; Liaise with the Procurement department as the case may be for the procurement of ZUSA’s goods and services; Avails ZUSA’s books of accounts to the University’s Internal Auditor; Presents audited financial statements to Congress at its last sitting before the AGM; Presents an audited annual financial report to the AGM; Serves as the custodian of ZUSA’s investments and assets; Receives all monies paid to the Association and be responsible for the Union’s funds; Accept subscriptions and donations in the name of the Union with the approval of the executive council In constant consultation with the Finance committee give monthly financial expenditure and income report to Congress Submits an up-to-date semester budget in the first Congress sitting every semester Keep financial receipts and prepare a financial statement for the Annual General Meeting; Appoint and with the approval of Congress chair the finance committee in Congress An ex-official member in any ad hoc committee dealing with any financial matter

Academic Secretary

Participates in the planning, organization and management of academic, social, counseling, career and work study programme; Articulates the special academic needs, aspirations and interests of ZUSA members Collects views from students on academic issues and present them to executive council meetings and Congress for appropriate action; Proposes by-laws and policies related to academic affairs Represents ZUSA in any other relevant committees dealing with academic issues; Represents ZUSA in disciplinary boards dealing with issues related to academics Liaise with the the DVC (academic affairs) and the registrar on academic issues; Work in liaison with the University Librarian and be a representative in the Senate Library and Bookshop Committee, Time-Tabling Committee and Board of Common Undergraduate Courses; Appoint and with the approval of Congress chair the Academic Affairs Committee as stipulated under this Constitution; .

Vice Chaiperson

The principal assistant to the chairperson and deputize the chairperson in the his roles and functions Performs the functions of the chairperson as the chairperson may assign or as may be assigned by the ZUSA Congress Acts as the chairperson in the absence of the chairperson or when the chairperson is incapacitated

Sports & Entertainment Secretary

Liaise with the Office of the Dean of Students and the University Sports Department to ensure members’ welfare in matters pertaining to sports and entertainment Develops Sports and Entertainment policy of the association. Represents ZUSA in all University Sports Council and all Sports Department Committees; Represents ZUSA in the management of the University Sports Teams; Organize, plan and facilitate the implementation of CSR activities inside and outside the University Oversee the implementation of all ZUSA activities related to sports and entertainment Directly answerable to ZUSA executive council and Zusa Congress, on any matter directly/indirectly related to Sports and entertainment activities of the members of the Association; Keeps detailed and exhaustive records of all sports and Entertainment activities; Ensures that there is orderliness during any entertainment event organized or hosted by the Association; Organizes and hosts all forms of entertainment approved by the executive; and on approval by Dean of Students and the DVC Academic Affairs; In consultation with the Sports and Entertainment Committee prepare budget to cater for the sporting and Entertainment activities within the limits of the activity fee; Prepare a financial documents and present it to Congress in the first sitting in every semester showing the expenditure on entertainment events held the previous semester as well as returns from the same events. Coordinates and promote sports and entertainment activities in the UNIVERSITY and communicate to members the external and internal fixtures; Ensures provision of cultural and implementation of programmes concerning sports and entertainment;

Social Welfare & Clubs Secretary

Represent ZUSA at the student welfare committee of the senate and catering and accommodation service board of the University; Main consultative person to the ZUSA Congress on any matter related directly or indirectly to Health, Catering and Accommodation of members of the Association; Supervises all ZUSA activities directly related to gender and in particular, network with relevant organizations on gender issues Promote gender equity in ZUSA and ZETECH University Liaise with the appropriate offices of the university in disseminating information among members about the university’s gender and sexual harassment policies and in particular keep the executive council informed on the implementation of the policies Receive and record members request for financial assistance and in particular request related to matters needy students Coordinate social welfare services to members and in particular organize for transport and benevolent at time for bereavement and related services Appoint and with the approval of Congress be the chairperson in the social welfare committee in ZUSA Congress Liaise with the UNIVERSITY Catering Manager for the provision of affordable meals to members of the Association within the university dining hall; Ensure all members of the Association receive valuable and quality medical assistance from the UNIVERSITY Dispensary; In liaison with the Dean of Students Office, ensure that members of the association live in proper and habitable facilities; Be the custodian of all records of communication related to Health, Catering and Accommodation of the Association; Ensure that no fully registered member of the Association lacks accommodation and or is marginalized in terms of Health, Catering and Accommodation; Research and disseminate information on programs, facilities and services available to persons with special needs
