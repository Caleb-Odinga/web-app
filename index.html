LET'S CONNECT
The Constitution

Set of fundamental laws and regulations or established precedents according to which ZUSA is governed that helps in guidance. .

ZUSA Congress

The official ZUSA legislative body, comprising the executive council, all elected Campus chairpersons, elected members from each Campus assembly, The Speaker, The Deputy Speaker, two clerks, two sergeants at arm and any other member(s), nominated or otherwise in accordance with this constitution.

The Campus Assembly

Organization of the representatives of a given campus. It comprises of all class representatives, club and society officials who study and reside in that particular campus. The assembly is headed by the campus chairperson and it escalates its concerns to the congress. It enhances effective unit representation .

The Executive Council

Comprises of seven offices, of which six are directly elected by the electoral college. It has the office of the Chairperson, Vice Chairperson(which must be of the opposite gender), Secretary General,Finance Secretary, Academic Secretary, Sports & Entertainment Secretary and the office of the Social Welfare and Clubs Secretary who must be a female. These leaders are ever committed to ensure comrades are comfortable in all spheres.

About Us

Welcome to ZUSA

Welcome to ZUSA experience, where the love of service and community comes together in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Zetech University Students Association was founded in the year 2014 to champion for the welfare of students. It was formed to ensure the students get support in their studies by being the link between the students and the administration. To serve the student community in the pursuit of academic and social welfare, peace, prosperity and integrity: and to have forums for discussion and debates, for the personal development of the students’ fraternity.
